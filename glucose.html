Glucose is critical in our body, pancreas help us to regulate it. When our body needs glucose, glucagon would be release by the pancreas. The effect of glucagon is to make the liver release the glucose it has stored in its cells into the bloodstream, with the net effect of increasing blood glucose. Our bodies desire blood glucose to be maintained between 70 mg/dl and 110 mg/dl.
